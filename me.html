Hi there! My name is George Hawk (he/him/his) and I am currently a math teacher at St. Isidore school in Danville, CA. As a graduate from Oberlin College with majors in Mathematics and Computer Science, not only do I enjoy teaching math, but I enjoy using math to create programs. One of my favorite ways to do this is by making art using programs. Some of the math art that I have made can be found on the art page of this website, and some of the math art that my students have made can be found here as well. When I'm not programming, I also enjoy playing ultimate frisbee.
