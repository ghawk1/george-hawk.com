My name is George Hawk (he/him/his). After graduatuating from Oberlin College with majors in Mathematics and Computer Science, I taught math for a year at St. Isidore School in Danville, CA. One way I love to combine my majors as well as my love for art is by creating programs that produce math art. Some of the art I've made as well as some art I facilitated my students in making can be found on this website. I also love music, ultimate frisbee, and being part of the LGBTQIA+ community. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to reach out :)
